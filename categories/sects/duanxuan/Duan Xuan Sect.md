# Duan Xuan Sect

Duan Xuan Sect splits into several unique archetypes. These include:
- Crash Fist
- Force Gain
- Physique
- Self-Debuff

These archetypes can be played together and are piloted in different ways depending on the character you are using.

---

## The Duan Xuan Sect consists of 5 characters. 

- **Xiao Bu**

- **Tu Kui**

- **Ye Mingming**

- [**Ji Fangsheng**](/categories/sects/duanxuan/Ji%20Fangsheng)

- **Li Man**
